Enrolment Process Overview

Student Applies to Enrol:

  • On our Resources page you will find the Enrolment paperwork required to commence a course at Australian Training Company.
  • The Resources page also includes important information that students must be aware of prior to enrolment such as: Fees, Refund Policy; Privacy Policy; USI requirements; Consumer Protection Policy and Support Services.

Check Performed for Government Subsidised Program Eligibility

  • With your permission ATC are required to notify the relevant government department of your details and your request to enrol in a subsidised program. The Enrolment paperwork includes information on this consent.

Induction

  • ATC will then send you an email concerning your Course Induction. Included in this email will be information on the provision of ID Documents, USI requirements and an Initial Skills Assessment you will need to submit. Your enrolment cannot be approved until these are completed.
  • This will take place prior to course commencement. Your trainer will conduct the induction and go through all the course requirements and resources available to you as the student. This may include unit selection and RPL where applicable.

Training Plans

  • A Training Plan will be developed that confirms the content of your course and the dates that assignments are due. All parties to the course/traineeship are required to sign this document.
