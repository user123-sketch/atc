About Our RTO

Australian Training Company established an RTO in 1998 in response to increasing demands for tailored education programs. ATC’s training operation promotes flexible study arrangements and an inclusive learning culture.


Government Funding

ATC's RTO operates state funded training in the following states:

  • NSW
  • ACT
  • QLD

Covid-19 Message

Australian Training Company is continuing to support our Students, Trainees and Employers during this difficult time for all Australia.

To promote social distancing and ensure the safety of our Staff, Students and Visitors, we have closed our training centres and students are working with trainers remotely. Staff, where practical are also working remotely. Australian Training Company is offering a full range of distant learning support and services, working with students on an individual basis to modify practical assessments whilst maintaining the integrity of our training and assessment methods.

If you want to emerge from this pause job ready or ready to start your own business please contact us to find out how we can help you make that happen.


Training Centre Facilities

Australian Training Company is a nationally Registered Training Organisation (RTO), approved by the Australian Skills Quality Authority (ASQA) RTO#5673. Our programs meet the strict guidelines set out by State and Federal Governments and their training bodies.

Our trainers are qualified professionals in their field of expertise, having studied and worked for a number of years to gain skills and experience as well as being trained trainers who undertake ongoing professional development to maintain currency and industry standards.

Training and assessment is approved for delivery across the country, with state government funding available in NSW, QLD and ACT.
